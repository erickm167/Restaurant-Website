Our Story








The Burger Barn began in 1995 when high-school friends Erick and Joshua decided they were tired of boring burgers. With a small grill and an old food cart, they started selling their fun, flavorful smash burgers in a parking lot behind their school. At first things were slow, but once people tried the burgers — each with its own quirky name — word spread fast. What started as a tiny stand soon became a local favorite. Today, The Burger Barn has grown to over 100 locations across the U.S., still serving the creative burgers that made it popular in the first place.
